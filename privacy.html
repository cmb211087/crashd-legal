2. Our approach: private by design

Crashd is built to keep your data on your device and in your own Apple iCloud. We do not run any servers or backend of our own. Your information is stored on your device and synced (if you enable iCloud) through Apple's private CloudKit — a space only you can access. We cannot see it.

We do not sell your data. We do not use third-party advertising or tracking SDKs. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

4. Apple Health (HealthKit)

Crashd reads caffeine you've logged in Apple Health to compute your daily total. Crashd writes the caffeine in drinks you log to Apple Health so you have a single source of truth. If you enable it, Crashd also reads sleep data on demand to show your personal caffeine-and-sleep correlation.

Your HealthKit data never leaves your device. Crashd does not transmit, share, or store your HealthKit data anywhere outside of Apple Health and your device's local storage. We never copy HealthKit data into our iCloud/CloudKit container. You can grant or revoke this access at any time in the Health app or iOS Settings; the app works without it.

5. Subscriptions (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat, Inc. (USA) to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives an anonymous app-user identifier and your Apple purchase/transaction data so we can grant and restore Crashd Pro. It does not receive your name or your health data. Payment itself is handled by Apple — we never see your card details. RevenueCat acts as our processor; data may be processed in the United States under appropriate safeguards (section 9).

6. The AI coach (Apple Foundation Models)

The coach runs on Apple's on-device Foundation Models. Your conversations with the coach are generated and stored on your device only. They are not sent to us, to Apple's servers, or to any third-party AI service. We do not use a remote AI provider. Coach replies are marked as AI-generated, and you'll see a one-time consent screen before your first chat. On devices that don't support Apple Intelligence, the coach falls back to fixed, pre-written messages with no AI processing.

7. What we do not do

  • No advertising or analytics tracking SDKs; no cross-app/-site tracking ("Data Used to Track You: None").
  • No selling or sharing of personal data for others' marketing.
  • No remote/third-party AI; no developer-run backend.
  • No collection of your camera frames or your HealthKit data off-device.
