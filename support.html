Common questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed by Apple. Manage or cancel in iPhone Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions. Deleting the app does not cancel a subscription. Refunds are handled by Apple.

How do I restore a purchase?

On the paywall or in Settings → Subscription, tap Restore Purchases while signed in with the Apple ID you bought with.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app: Settings → Account → Delete Account. This wipes your streak, logs, journal entries, and synced data across your devices. An active subscription continues until you cancel it in iPhone Settings.

Does Crashd see my health data?

No. Caffeine and sleep data from Apple Health are read on your device only and never leave it. See the Privacy Policy.

Is the coach a real person?

No. The coach is generated on-device by Apple's Foundation Models and is marked as AI. It is not medical advice — see the Terms. In an emergency, contact your local emergency services.
